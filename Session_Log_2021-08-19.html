1 Unholy Clue-DND Hybrid

This session was special, and mainly consisted of playing Clue with interruptions from DND. We had a stand-in for Ezra, but it ultimately didn't matter much. There was very little roleplaying involved. Dearest DM held some cards, but the rules were unclear, which ultimately resulted in me making some bad deductions. Our first two accusations were actually wrong, since another player actually had the same problem.

At the end of the session we discovered that Atlas was the culprit, and also a shapeshifter.
